# SmartPages – Product Roadmap

This roadmap gives a **high-level overview** of how SmartPages is evolving.

It is intended for **orientation and transparency**, not as a fixed delivery promise. Priorities may shift based on user feedback and market needs.

---

## Phase 1 – Foundation (Current)

### SmartProfile

The first core module of SmartPages.

Focus:
- professional profile pages
- clear structure for personal or business presence
- fast setup without technical knowledge
- solid foundation for all other modules

Status: **In progress**

---

## Phase 2 – Expansion

### SmartLinks

Build on top of SmartProfile to manage links more flexibly.

Focus:
- link collections ("link in bio")
- clean redirects
- simple structure for creators and small businesses

Status: **Planned**

---

### SmartPage

Lightweight pages for content beyond profiles and links.

Focus:
- simple landing pages
- offers, information, campaigns
- no traditional website complexity

Status: **Planned**

---

## Phase 3 – Monetisation & Infrastructure (Summer)

### Payments (TBD)

Possible monetisation features.

Examples:
- paid profiles or pages
- premium features
- subscriptions

Status: **Under evaluation**

---

### SmartDomain

Custom domains as an optional upgrade.

Focus:
- connect own domains
- improve brand trust
- keep setup simple

Status: **Under evaluation**

---

## Long-Term Direction

In later stages, SmartPages may expand towards:

- improved analytics and insights
- multi-profile management
- workflows for agencies

These topics are intentionally not prioritised early to keep the product focused and simple.

---

## Guiding Principles

- start small, build solid
- focus on real user problems
- avoid unnecessary complexity
- grow the platform step by step

---

This roadmap represents the **current direction** of SmartPages and may evolve over time.
